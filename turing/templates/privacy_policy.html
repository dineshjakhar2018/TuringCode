Last updated: 22-01-2024

d. User Engagement: The data collected helps us analyze user behavior, track engagement patterns, and improve our platform's functionality, content, and features.

4. Security Measures

a. Data Security: Turing Code employs robust security measures to protect your personal information. This includes encryption, secure connections, and regular security audits.

b. User Responsibilities: Users are encouraged to protect their login credentials and promptly notify us of any unauthorized use of their account.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy or this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@turingcode.in.

Thank you for choosing Turing Code as your online study platform. Your trust is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your privacy and providing you with an exceptional learning experience.
