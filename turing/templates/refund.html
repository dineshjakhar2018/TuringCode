Last updated: 22-01-2024

Turing Code is committed to providing a satisfactory and enriching learning experience to all our users. This Refund/Cancellation Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds and cancellations are processed for our services.

a. Course Enrollment Fees: Refunds may be considered for course enrollment fees under the following circumstances:

b. Cancellation of Services: Turing Code reserves the right to cancel a course or service at any time due to unforeseen circumstances. In such cases, users will be entitled to a full refund.

2. Refund Process

a. To request a refund, users must contact our support team at contact@turingcode.in within the specified refund period, providing details of the reason for the refund request.

b. Refund requests will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis, and approval is at the discretion of Turing Code.

c. If a refund is approved, the refund amount and method will be determined by Turing Code.

3. Cancellation Policy

a. User-Requested Cancellations: Users may request to cancel their course enrollment within 3 days of purchase. In such cases, a full refund may be granted.

b. Automatic Cancellations: Turing Code reserves the right to cancel a user's enrollment if there is a violation of the terms of service or any inappropriate conduct on the platform.

4. Contact Us

For any questions or concerns regarding our Refund/Cancellation Policy, please contact us at contact@turingcode.in.

Turing Code reserves the right to update and modify this Refund/Cancellation Policy. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on the Site. Users are encouraged to review this policy regularly for the latest information.
